About Us - Potty Spotty

Founded by a small and passionate group of college students from Harold Washington College—Anna Gerberich, Brandon Smith, Larissa Quiroz, and Luis Luna—We recognize that the call of nature can strike at any time. Our mission was to develop a user-friendly platform dedicated to assisting both locals and tourists in locating clean and accessible public bathrooms wherever their journeys take them. Our collective vision is to simplify the search for public bathrooms in major cities, ensuring that people can readily find facilities that are open to the public. At Potty Spotty, we value community engagement and encourage the general public to contribute by submitting comments, suggestions, and feedback to help us keep our information current and reliable. Together, we aim to enhance the overall bathroom-finding experience, promoting convenience and accessibility for everyone.
