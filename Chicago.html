1. The Rookery Building

Frank Lloyd Wright redesigned the building, incorporating cool angles and marble covers for the ornately
designed pipes, resulting in exquisite attention to detail. Additionally, there is a souvenir store dedicated to
Frank Lloyd Wright within the building.

Address: 209 S LaSalle St., Chicago, IL
Hours of Operation: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

2. Harold Washington Library

The library is a grand building with nine floors to discover. At the very top, you will find the winter garden, which
boasts a stunning glass ceiling or dome. Each floor offers plenty of seating options for your comfort. The first floor
lobby is particularly striking and also provides access to wifi.

Address: 400 S State St., Chicago, IL
Hours of Operation: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

3. Skydeck located in Willis Tower

Gain entry to 360 Chicago (John Hancock Observatory) and marvel at the towering structure that dominates
Chicago’s skyline. Experience a rapid ascent of 94 storeys in the lightning-fast elevator to reach the observation
deck of one of North America’s tallest buildings. While there, you can also purchase tickets for TILT, a unique thrill ride
that offers visitors an opportunity to gaze downward from a height of 1,030 feet.

Address: 233 S Wacker Dr., Chicago, IL
Hours of Operation: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

4. Millennium Park

The park is an excellent destination for tourists and locals alike, offering a wealth of attractions such as the iconic
Cloud Gate (Bean) and Crown Fountain. A pathway also leads to Maggie Daley park, which features a variety of
activities for both young and old. During summer, the Millennium Park is a hub of activity with numerous events,
concerts, and festivals taking place throughout the season.

Address: 201 E Randolph St., Chicago, IL
Hours of Operation: 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

5. Wicker Park

The park covers over four acres and is of a decent size. Wicker Park contains basketball courts and a vast green
lawn. On the west side of the park, there are picnic tables available for use. The field house located in the park has a
gym and community rooms, making it a lively destination for visitors and events.

Address: 1425 N Damen Ave., Chicago, IL
Hours of Operation: 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible
