Miami Public Restrooms

1. Palm Court, Design District

You can find the most impressive public restrooms in Miami's Design by heading up the
escalator and turning right in the Palm Court. These restrooms are equipped with modern facilities and are always kept in impeccable condition.

Address: 140 NE 39th St., Miami, FL
Hours of Operation: 9:00 AM - 10 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

2. Lummus Park on South Beach

Three restrooms are available at the park, located on 6th, 10th, and 14th streets. South Beach houses a park spanning 74 acres,
stretching ten blocks from 5th Street to 14th Place. One side of the park is set against the famous Ocean Drive, with the other facing the Atlantic Ocean.
The park is trendy among runners, walkers, and cyclists enjoying the paved exercise path.

Address: 10th St. & Ocean Dr., Miami, FL
Hours of Operation: 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

3. Ron Ehmann Park

The park's offerings include a soccer field, basketball court, softball field, bike path, relaxing area, playground, outdoor fitness equipment,
recreation center, tennis center and six well-lit tennis courts. Despite being located in the urban Miami area, this park provides visitors with
a peaceful natural environment. Whether you're looking to play sports, exercise, observe birds or simply enjoy the surroundings, this nature
trail is the perfect place to do so.

Address: 10995 SW 97 Ave., Miami, FL
Hours of Operation: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

4. Downtown Doral Park

The park offers various amenities such as vast open lawns, a playground with shaded areas, a footpath surrounding an oval, and picnic tables.
You can enjoy the view of the Doral Government Center's light show in the evening as the park is located adjacent to it. Furthermore, the city
utilizes Downtown Doral Park to organize events like movie night and also decorates it for various holidays.

Address: 8395 NW 53th St., Miami, FL
Hours of Operation: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible

5. A.D. Barnes Park

The park provides various amenities, such as a swimming pool that typically hosts swimming lessons for children and fitness classes for seniors,
a lake for fishing, covered pavilions, tables, grills, a nature trail, a nature center, and an open grassy area. Additionally, the park comprises
a 15-acre Pine Rockland habitat.

Address: 3401 SW 72nd Ave., Miami, FL
Hours of Operation: 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Amenities: Baby changing station, wheelchair accessible
